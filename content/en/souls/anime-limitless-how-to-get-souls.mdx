<Section icon={Shield} title="Managing and Swapping Your Souls">
Once you've acquired multiple Souls, managing them becomes important. You can store extra Souls and, more importantly, reset your currently equipped Soul to try out new ones.

| Action        | NPC/Location | Cost         | Purpose                                     |
|:--------------|:-------------|:-------------|:--------------------------------------------|
| Acquire Soul  | Soul Dealer  | 25,000 Gold  | Get a random new Soul                       |
| Acquire Soul  | Chests       | Free         | Discover Souls through exploration          |
| Store Souls   | Inventory    | N/A          | Keep extra Souls for future use or giveaways|
| Swap Souls    | Soul Reset   | 150 Gems     | Change your active Soul                     |
| Awaken Soul   | West City    | N/A          | Unlock full potential of specific Souls     |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Store Unwanted Souls">
    You can store extra Souls in your inventory. Note that there might be a limit to how many you can store (e.g., two Souls).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Soul Reset NPC">
    Head back to **West City** and find the **Soul Reset** NPC. This NPC is essential for changing your equipped Soul.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reset Your Soul">
    Interact with the Soul Reset NPC. It will cost **150 Gems** to reset your current Soul. This action typically removes the Soul from your hand, allowing you to equip another.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip a New Soul">
    After resetting, you can then "eat" or equip the desired Soul from your inventory to activate its abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>
