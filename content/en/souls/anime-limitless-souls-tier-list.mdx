The two current kings of the meta require specific Rifts to reach their full potential. SSJ4 Goku excels in single-target destruction, making him the go-to for Boss farming. Kid Buu, however, offers superior crowd control, which is essential for clearing waves in the Abyss.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain and Evolve Souls">

Acquiring your first high-tier soul is a multi-step process. You cannot simply buy the best souls; you must earn them through a combination of luck and boss grinding.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Soul Dealer">
    Head to **West City**. Locate the Soul Dealer NPC. Each roll costs **25,000 Gold**. This is your primary method for obtaining Common through Legendary souls during the early and mid-game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Boss Chest Farming">
    Bosses have a chance to drop Chests upon defeat. Even **Common Chests** have a slim chance to contain a Legendary Soul. Higher-tier bosses drop better chests with increased Soul drop rates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locating Rifts">
    Once you have a Legendary Soul, you must find its corresponding Rift. The **SSJ4 Goku Rift** is on Truffle Island, while the **Kid Buu Rift** is tucked away in a corner of West City.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Soul Awakening">
    Enter the Rift and defeat the bosses inside to collect **Cores** and evolution items. Talk to the Awakener NPC located near the Rift entrance to finalize the transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Evolution Item | Source | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Goku Core** | SSJ4 Rift | Awakens SSJ4 Goku |
| **Buu Core** | Kid Buu Rift | Awakens Kid Buu |
| **Soul Gems** | Quests / Bosses | Used for Soul Resets |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Resetting your soul costs **150 Gems**. Only use this if you have a superior soul waiting in your inventory, as gems are a limited resource earned primarily through difficult quests.
</Callout>
