<Section icon={Swords} title="Awakening Legendary Souls: Goku SSJ4 & Kid Buu">
Awakening legendary souls is where their true power is unleashed, granting you access to unique transformations and devastating abilities. Currently, only Super Saiyan 4 Goku and Kid Buu have awakening forms, making them highly sought after. The process involves locating specific Rifts and their respective Awakeners.

Rifts are special areas that house powerful bosses and provide the necessary items to evolve or awaken your souls. These are crucial for endgame progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Rift">
    Identify the correct Rift for your desired legendary soul. The **Goku SSJ4 Rift** is on Truffle Island, and the **Kid Buu Rift** is in West City.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Rift & Farm">
    Inside the Rift, you'll encounter bosses. Defeat them to obtain specific items, cores, or accessories required for awakening. These items are vital for the awakening process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Awakener NPC">
    After collecting the necessary materials, seek out the Awakener NPC. For **Goku SSJ4**, the Awakener is on Truffle Island, directly in front of the quest giver. For **Kid Buu**, the Awakener is at the opposite side of the Kid Buu Rift in West City.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Initiate Awakening">
    Interact with the Awakener NPC and use the collected items to awaken your legendary soul. This will unlock its full potential, including a powerful transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Awakened Soul Benefits">
Awakened legendary souls provide significant stat boosts, new skills (Z, X, C moves), and a complete visual transformation, drastically increasing your combat effectiveness.
</Callout>
