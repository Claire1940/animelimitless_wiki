<Section icon={Swords} title="Farming the Best Souls: Methods and Locations">
Acquiring the **anime limitless best souls**, especially legendary ones, requires dedicated farming. There are two primary methods to get souls, each with its own advantages and challenges. Mastering these methods will ensure a steady supply of powerful souls for your character.

### Method 1: Boss Chest Drops
Bosses across various islands have a chance to drop chests, which can contain souls of different rarities. While common chests might yield a soul, legendary souls are a much rarer drop. This method encourages active combat and exploration.

<Callout type="warning" title="RNG Factor">
Relying solely on boss chest drops can be inconsistent due to the random nature of drops. Be prepared for a grind, as legendary souls have a very low drop rate.
</Callout>

### Method 2: Soul Dealer
The Soul Dealer, located in West City, offers another way to acquire souls. For a cost of 25,000 in-game currency per attempt, you can roll for a random soul. This method provides a more direct, albeit still RNG-dependent, approach to getting souls.

<Section icon={Target} title="Soul Management and Advanced Tips">
Beyond just acquiring souls, effective management and strategic use are vital. This includes knowing how to reset your soul, understanding key settings for grinding, and preparing for the ultimate challenge: The Abyss.

### Resetting Your Soul
If you wish to change your soul's stats or re-specialize, you can reset it. This costs gems and is an important feature for optimizing your build.

<Callout type="info" title="Soul Reset Location">
To reset your soul, head to West City. On the left side of the main building, you'll find a Soul Reset NPC. The cost is **150 gems**. Gems are typically acquired from quests and boss fights.
</Callout>

### Essential Grinding Settings
For efficient soul and stat grinding, enable "Auto M1s" in your game settings. This automatically attacks, allowing you to passively farm stats for your equipped soul or weapon. Additionally, the "Auto Farm" feature lets you select a skill to use continuously, ideal for overnight grinding.

### The Abyss: End-Game Challenge
The Abyss is the ultimate challenge in Anime Limitless, designed for end-game players. To enter, you need a total power of 3,000 (combined stats). Inside, you'll face a formidable boss that drops the best armor in the game and find a crafter for what is believed to be the best weapon.
