<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Souls, Rifts, and Transformations">

Souls are the heart of your power in Anime Limitless. Currently, there are four rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary. The two most sought-after souls are **SSJ4 Goku** and **Kid Buu**. These are not just stat sticks; they provide unique movesets (Z, X, and C moves) and powerful transformations.

**Obtaining Souls:**
1. **Chests**: Bosses have a chance to drop chests. Even common chests can occasionally contain a soul.
2. **Soul Dealer**: Located in West City, you can purchase a random soul for 25,000 Cash.

**Rifts and Awakening:**
Rifts are specialized instances where you fight high-level bosses to obtain evolution items. If you possess a legendary soul, you must visit its specific rift to "awaken" it, unlocking its full transformation potential.

| Legendary Soul | Rift Location | Awakener Location |

| **Kid Buu** | West City (Corner) | Opposite side of West City |

**How to Unlock Observation Haki:**
Unlocking Observation is a two-step process. First, you must have Armament Haki and train your Haki stat to at least 200. Then, head to Desert Island and find the Katakuri NPC. You will enter a trial where you must dodge the boss 10 times using the observation mode provided. Once successful, the ability is yours to keep and train.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Mastery">
