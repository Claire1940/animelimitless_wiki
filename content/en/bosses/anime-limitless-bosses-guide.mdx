<Section icon={Shield} title="Essential Boss Preparation Steps">
Before you even consider engaging a boss, thorough preparation can significantly increase your chances of success and make your farming runs more efficient. Rushing into boss fights without adequate stats or a optimized build will only lead to frustration and wasted time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Train Your Stats">
    Focus on increasing your primary combat stats (e.g., Strength, Durability). Use the active code **RELEASE** for 20 minutes of x2 Stat EXP to accelerate this process. Active grinding through combat is key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Your Soul">
    Check for available Souls during their 30-minute spawn window. A powerful Soul can dramatically change your combat effectiveness and is a core part of your build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Refine Race and Trait Rolls">
    Utilize any **Race/Trait Spins** you have. Aim for high-rarity rolls that complement your chosen combat style. The **LIMITLESSGIFT** community reward provides 5 Race/Trait Spins, which are invaluable early on.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Acquire Haki">
    Haki is a late-game combat system that provides significant offensive and defensive boosts. Armament Haki enhances attacks and durability, while Observation Haki improves evasion. Focus on Haki once your basic stats are stable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Understand Boss Mechanics">
    Before the fight, try to learn the boss's attack patterns, weaknesses, and any special abilities. This can often be found through community resources or by observing other players.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Rush">
Attempting bosses too early without proper preparation is a common mistake. It depletes resources and slows overall progression. Ensure your build is ready.
</Callout>

</Section>
