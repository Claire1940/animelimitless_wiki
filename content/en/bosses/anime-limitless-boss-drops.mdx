<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Boss Farming Strategies">
Efficiently farming bosses requires a systematic approach. Once you are adequately prepared, establishing a repeatable farming loop will yield the best results for acquiring **anime limitless boss drops**. This involves not just defeating the boss, but also understanding respawn timers and optimizing your build for speed and consistency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Your Build and Target Boss">
    Before each farming session, review your current stats, equipped Soul, Race, Traits, and Haki levels. Choose a boss that you can consistently defeat with minimal difficulty. Do not chase bosses that are too strong, as it will slow down your farming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate EXP Boosts and Buffs">
    Redeem any active codes for x2 Stat EXP or other relevant buffs (if available). These boosts will not only help with general stat training but also make your boss clears faster and more efficient, indirectly increasing your drop rate per hour.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Consistent Boss Runs">
    Focus on repeating the boss fight as quickly and safely as possible. Learn the boss's attack patterns and develop a rotation that minimizes damage taken while maximizing your output. Consistency is more important than raw speed if it means fewer failed attempts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Track Drops and Inventory">
    Regularly check your inventory for acquired **anime limitless boss drops**, especially Boss Essence and unique materials. Keep track of what you need and adjust your farming target if necessary. This prevents unnecessary grinding for items you already have.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evaluate and Upgrade Your Build">
    After a farming session, assess whether your accumulated drops allow for significant build upgrades. Invest in new abilities, stronger gear, or improved Souls/Races/Traits. Each upgrade should ideally make subsequent boss runs even more efficient.
  </Step>
</Steps>
