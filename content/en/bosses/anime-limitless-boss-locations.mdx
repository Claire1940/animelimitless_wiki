<Section icon={Target} title="Preparing for Boss Encounters">
Effective boss farming requires careful preparation. Rushing into a boss fight without adequate stats or a refined build can lead to wasted time and resources. Focus on these key areas before attempting to take down tougher enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Train Your Stats">
    Before engaging bosses, actively train your stats through combat. Utilize any available x2 Stat EXP rewards to accelerate this process. Consistent stat growth is the foundation for handling stronger enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Souls and Traits">
    Ensure your current Soul, Race, and Trait setup is optimized for combat. Souls provide powerful abilities, while Races and Traits offer passive bonuses that can significantly impact your damage output and survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Haki">
    Haki is a vital combat system that enhances your character's offensive and defensive capabilities. Armament Haki improves attack power and durability, while Observation Haki enhances awareness and evasion, making boss fights much more manageable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Community Rewards">
    Redeem the `LIMITLESSGIFT` key from the Community Reward Gojo NPC in West City. This code provides 3 Boss Essences and 5 Race/Trait Spins, giving you a head start on crucial resources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
Always activate any available EXP boosts, such as the `RELEASE` code for x2 Stat EXP, before starting a grinding or boss farming session. This maximizes your efficiency and speeds up progression.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Farming Strategies and Loops">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Track Drop Rates">
    Keep an eye on drop rates for specific items you need. Some bosses might be better for certain materials than others. The official Trello or Discord might provide community-sourced drop data.
  </Card>
