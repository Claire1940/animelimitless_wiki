Souls are a major ability and build system, impacting your character's performance. For raids, prioritize Souls that offer damage amplification, crowd control, or strong defensive capabilities.

| Soul Type     | Primary Benefit                    | Raid Application                                 | Priority   |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Raid Boss Mechanics and Combat Tips">
Each raid boss in Anime Limitless will have unique attack patterns, special abilities, and vulnerabilities. Understanding these is crucial for effective combat.

### General Combat Flow for Raids
The core loop remains consistent: fight enemies, use abilities, and manage your resources. However, in raids, timing and positioning become far more critical.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement & Debuffs">
    Start the fight by applying any debuffs your build allows (e.g., from certain Souls or abilities). This weakens the boss for subsequent attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Damage Phase & Ability Rotation">
    Unleash your main damage abilities, focusing on optimal rotation to maximize DPS. Pay attention to boss tells for incoming attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defensive Maneuvers & Haki Use">
    Actively dodge or block boss attacks. Utilize Observation Haki for incoming danger and Armament Haki to mitigate damage or break through defenses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Management & Sustain">
    Monitor your health and energy. Use potions or healing abilities strategically to maintain sustain throughout the fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adapt to Phase Changes">
    Raid bosses often have multiple phases, changing their attack patterns or gaining new abilities. Adapt your strategy accordingly and focus down new threats (adds).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Haki in Raids">
Haki is invaluable in raids. **Armament Haki** enhances your offensive capabilities and reduces incoming damage, while **Observation Haki** allows you to predict and dodge critical boss attacks, significantly increasing your survivability. Don't neglect upgrading these.
</Callout>
