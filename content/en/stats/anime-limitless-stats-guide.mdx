<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Stat Farming Locations & NPCs">

Efficient stat farming relies heavily on knowing where to go and which NPCs to interact with. The game features an **Island Tracker NPC** on the Starter Island, which can help guide you to the best spots for your current level and stat goals.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Starter Island: Core Services">
    Begin your journey on the Starter Island. Locate the **Island Tracker NPC** here, which is invaluable for guiding you to suitable farming locations across different islands. Most islands also feature merchants for essential items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Monkey Island: Early Melee & Questing">
    Head to Monkey Island for early melee training. You'll find the **Axehand Seller** and the starting point for the **Armament Haki quest line**, which can provide significant combat bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Arlong Park: Fisherman Karate">
    Visit Arlong Park to find the **Fisherman Karate Seller**. This is an excellent stop for players focusing on strength-based builds or looking to diversify their combat styles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Desert Island: Black Leg & Observation Haki">
    The Desert Island is crucial for players focusing on agility and observation. Here, you'll find the **Black Leg Seller** and the **Observation Haki Seller**, both offering powerful enhancements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="West City: Soul Dealers & Awakener">
    West City is a hub for advanced power-ups. It houses the **Soul Dealer**, **Soul Reset NPC**, and the **Kid Buu Awakener**. You'll also find the **Kidoo Rift NPC** and the **Boss Arena** for challenging encounters and rare drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Truffle Island: Super Saiyan 4 Goku Awakener">
    For those seeking ultimate power, Truffle Island is home to the **Super Saiyan 4 Goku Awakener**. This transformation is one of the most potent souls in the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="NPC Location Summary">
Knowing the locations of key NPCs is as important as knowing where to fight. Regularly check the **Island Tracker NPC** on Starter Island for updated information on optimal farming spots and quest givers.
