### Optimizing Your Grind

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Auto M1s and Auto Skills">
    Navigate to your game settings and enable "Auto M1s" and "Auto Skills." This is crucial for passive stat grinding, allowing your character to continuously attack and use skills, even overnight, to build up relevant stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Track Quests">
    The "Track Quest" feature on the left side of your screen helps identify optimal grinding locations for specific stats. For instance, if you want to level your Soul stat, it will guide you to the appropriate quest.

  <Step num="3" title="Stack EXP Boosts">

  <Step num="4" title="Efficient Health and Haki Training">
    Health stat increases by taking damage or by being a soul user in water. Haki levels up through active use of skills or weapons while it's equipped. Consistent use is key for both.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Haki Acquisition

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Armament Haki">
    Travel to Monkey Island and locate the Busoshoku trainer. You'll need 75 Stamina and 75 Strength or Sword to start the quest. Completing this quest grants you Armament Haki, which buffs your attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Observation Haki">
    First, you must have Armament Haki and train it to 200 stats. Then, head to Desert Island and find the Katakuri NPC. You'll need 200 Stamina and 200 Haki to begin his trial. The trial involves dodging attacks, and upon completion, you'll gain Observation Haki, allowing you to dodge incoming attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The true test of your power in **Anime Limitless gameplay** lies in its challenging end-game content: Rifts and The Abyss. These areas offer the best rewards, but demand significant preparation and power.

### Rifts: Legendary Item Farming

Rifts are specialized zones containing powerful bosses that drop items essential for evolving your souls, obtaining accessories, and collecting cores. There are currently two main rifts in the game:

| Rift Name        | Location      | Awakener Location         | Primary Drops                                |
| :--------------- | :------------ | :------------------------ | :------------------------------------------- |
| **Goku SSJ4 Rift** | Truffle Island | In front of quest giver   | SSJ4 awakening items, accessories, cores     |
| **Kid Buu Rift** | West City     | Opposite side of West City | Kid Buu awakening items, accessories, cores  |

<Callout type="tip" title="Rift Strategy">
Form a party when tackling rifts, especially the higher-tier ones. Coordination with teammates can make farming these challenging bosses much more efficient, increasing your chances of securing rare drops.
</Callout>

### The Abyss: The Ultimate Challenge

The Abyss represents the pinnacle of **Anime Limitless gameplay** for end-game players. It houses the strongest boss and offers the best armor and materials for crafting the most powerful weapon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Abyss Gatekeeper">
    Head to Truffle Island and climb to the highest point to find the Abyss Gatekeeper NPC.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Achieve 3,000 Total Power">
    The gatekeeper will only grant access if your total power level (sum of all your stats) reaches 3,000. This is a demanding requirement, signifying true mastery of the game's progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the Abyss">
    Inside, defeat the boss to earn the best armor in the game. You'll also find a crafter who can forge the ultimate weapon, requiring all available cores.
  </Step>
</Steps>
