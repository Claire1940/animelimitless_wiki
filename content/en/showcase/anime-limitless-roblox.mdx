### Unlocking Haki

Haki is a fundamental combat mechanic that significantly boosts your offensive and defensive capabilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Basic Haki">
    Travel to **Monkey Island**. Take the quest from the NPC there. You'll need at least 75 Stamina and 75 Strength or Sword to begin. Completing this quest grants you basic Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Observation Haki">
    Once your Haki level reaches 200, head to **Sand Island** (located behind the starter island). Speak to Kosakuri, who will guide you through a quick trial to learn Observation Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level Up Observation Haki">
    Observation Haki increases your dodge count. The more you use it and dodge attacks, the higher your dodge count will become, up to a maximum of nine dodges.
  </Step>
</Steps>
