<Section icon={Trophy} title="Leveling, Stats, and Soul Acquisition">
Progression in Anime Limitless is a blend of traditional leveling and a deep stat-based system. Each island offers quests that contribute to your player level, but the true power comes from diligently increasing your stats. These stats are crucial for unlocking new abilities, improving combat effectiveness, and meeting the requirements for advanced content.

### Understanding the Progression System

| Progression Aspect | Description | Key Benefit |
|:-------------------|:------------|:------------|
| **Player Level**   | Standard experience-based progression, unlocks new islands and quests. | Access to new content |
| **Stats**          | Increased by using specific combat types (melee, sword, gun, soul). | **Direct combat power, ability unlocks** |
| **Soul Stats**     | Specific quests boost soul-related stats. | Enhance soul abilities |

### How to Get Souls

Souls are a primary source of power, offering unique abilities and transformations. There are two main methods to acquire them:

| Method         | Location/Source | Cost/Requirement | Rarities Available |
|:---------------|:----------------|:-----------------|:-------------------|
| **Boss Drops** | Random drops from defeated bosses. | Free | Common, Rare, Epic, **Legendary** |
| **Soul Dealer**| West City       | 25,000 Gold per roll | Common, Rare, Epic, **Legendary** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Rarity Matters">
While common chests can drop souls, legendary souls like Super Saiyan 4 Goku are rare finds. The Soul Dealer offers a consistent, albeit costly, way to try for higher rarities.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Haki and Fighting Styles">
Haki is a vital combat mechanic in Anime Limitless, offering both offensive and defensive advantages. There are two types: Armament Haki and Observation Haki, each with distinct benefits. Additionally, two unique fighting styles can be learned to diversify your combat approach.

### Acquiring Haki

| Haki Type         | Requirements                                       | Location     | Process                                           |
|:------------------|:---------------------------------------------------|:-------------|:--------------------------------------------------|
| **Armament Haki** | 75 Stamina, 75 Strength/Sword Stat                 | Monkey Island | Talk to Busoshoku trainer, complete quest (10-20 mins) |
| **Observation Haki**| Armament Haki, 200 Haki Stat, 200 Stamina        | Desert Island | Talk to Katakuri, complete trial (get hit 10 times with observation active) |

<Callout type="success">
Observation Haki is an excellent defensive tool, allowing you to dodge attacks. Actively using it will train and improve its effectiveness over time.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Exploring Rifts and the Abyss">
Rifts are crucial endgame content in Anime Limitless, providing avenues to obtain powerful items for soul evolution and rare accessories. For the most dedicated players, the Abyss presents the ultimate challenge with the best gear in the game.

### Rift Mechanics and Locations

Rifts feature bosses that drop essential items for evolving souls, as well as accessories and cores. Currently, there are two legendary souls, each with a corresponding rift.

| Rift Type       | Location          | Awakener Location         | Primary Purpose                                   |
|:----------------|:------------------|:--------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------|
| **Kid Buu Rift**| West City (corner)| West City (opposite side) | Items for Kid Buu soul evolution, accessories, cores |
| **Goku SSJ4 Rift**| Truffle Island    | Truffle Island (in front of quest giver) | Items for SSJ4 Goku soul evolution, accessories, cores |

<Callout type="warning" title="Endgame Content">
Rifts are designed for players with well-developed stats and souls. Ensure you are adequately prepared before challenging these bosses to maximize your rewards and minimize frustration.
</Callout>

### The Abyss: The Ultimate Challenge

The Abyss is a high-level endgame zone intended for the strongest players. It houses a formidable boss that drops the best armor in the game and a crafter for the most powerful weapon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Abyss Gatekeeper">
    Travel to Truffle Island and climb to the designated area where the Abyss Gatekeeper NPC resides.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Meet Power Requirements">
    To enter, you need a **total power of 3,000**. This is the sum of all your stats. The Gatekeeper will inform you: "The abyss rejects the weak. Beyond this gate stands the one who took it all. Return when your power total reaches 3,000."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the Abyss">
    Inside, defeat the boss for the best armor and collect components to craft the best weapon, which often requires rare cores from throughout the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>
