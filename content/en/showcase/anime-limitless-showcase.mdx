<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Combat: Haki & Fighting Styles">
Combat in Anime Limitless is dynamic, offering various fighting styles and powerful Haki abilities. Understanding how to acquire and train these skills is crucial for dominating both PvE and PvP encounters. There are two main Haki types: Armament and Observation.

### Acquiring Armament Haki

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Requirements">
    Ensure you have at least **75 Stamina** and **75 Strength or Sword** stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to Monkey Island">
    Head to Monkey Island, the second island in the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find Busoshoku Trainer">
    Locate the Busoshoku Trainer NPC on Monkey Island.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Start the Quest">
    Initiate the quest offered by the trainer. This quest is generally straightforward and doesn't take long, typically 10-20 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Armament Haki is a prerequisite for obtaining Observation Haki. Make sure to train your Armament Haki to at least 200 stats before attempting to acquire Observation.
</Callout>

### Obtaining Observation Haki

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Train Armament Haki">
    Level your Armament Haki stat to **200**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Go to Desert Island">
    Travel to Desert Island, the third island in the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Katakuri NPC">
    Find the Katakuri NPC on Desert Island.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Meet Requirements & Start Trial">
    You will need **200 Stamina** and **200 Haki** stats. Talk to Katakuri to start the trial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Complete the Trial">
    During the trial, activate Observation Haki (given for free during the trial) and get hit 10 times by the boss. This is an easy task.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Souls are a primary source of power in Anime Limitless, with legendary souls offering significant boosts and unique transformations. Rifts serve as crucial endgame content, providing the necessary materials to awaken and enhance these powerful entities.

### How to Acquire Souls

| Method           | Location/Source        | Rarity Chance                 | Cost/Effort                                    |
|:-----------------|:-----------------------|:------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------|
| **Boss Chests**  | Dropped by bosses      | Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary | RNG dependent, requires boss farming           |

The game currently features two legendary souls: Super Saiyan 4 Goku and Kid Buu. Both are highly sought after for their combat prowess and transformation abilities.

### Understanding Rifts

Rifts are special zones containing powerful bosses that drop items essential for soul evolution, accessories, and cores. There are two rifts, each tied to a legendary soul.

| Rift Type         | Location         | Awakener NPC Location        | Primary Drops                                     |
|:------------------|:-----------------|:-----------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------|
| **Goku SSJ4 Rift** | Truffle Island   | In front of quest giver      | Items for SSJ4 awakening, accessories, cores      |
| **Kid Buu Rift**   | West City        | Opposite side of West City   | Items for Kid Buu awakening, accessories, cores   |

<Callout type="success">
Awakening a legendary soul like SSJ4 Goku grants a full transformation, unlocking powerful new moves and significantly increasing your combat effectiveness.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame Challenges: The Abyss">
The Abyss represents the ultimate challenge in Anime Limitless, designed exclusively for endgame players. It houses the toughest boss and offers the best armor and crafting materials for the most powerful weapons in the game.

To enter The Abyss, you must reach **3,000 total power**. This is calculated by summing all your individual stats. Reaching this threshold requires significant grinding across all aspects of the game.
