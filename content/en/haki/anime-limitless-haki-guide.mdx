<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking Armament Haki (Busoshoku)">
Armament Haki, also known as Busoshoku Haki, is your first step into the world of advanced combat. It allows you to imbue your body and weapons with a hard, invisible armor, increasing your attack power and defense.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Stat Requirements">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to Monkey Island">
    Once your stats meet the requirements, head to Monkey Island. This island is home to the Busoshoku Trainer, who will guide you in unlocking this powerful ability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Busoshoku Trainer">
    On Monkey Island, find the Busoshoku Trainer NPC. They are typically located in a prominent area, so keep an eye out as you explore.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Start the Quest">
    Speak with the trainer to initiate the Armament Haki quest. While the specific tasks may vary slightly with updates, they generally involve demonstrating your combat prowess or completing a short challenge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Complete the Quest">
    Follow the trainer's instructions and complete the quest. It's designed to be a relatively quick process, often taking around 10-20 minutes, even if you're new to it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Armament Haki Benefits">
Armament Haki provides a significant boost to your offensive and defensive capabilities. It's indispensable for fighting tougher enemies and for PvP encounters, allowing you to bypass certain defensive abilities and deal more damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Acquiring Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku)">
Observation Haki, or Kenbunshoku Haki, grants you enhanced senses, allowing you to foresee attacks and dodge them with incredible precision. This is a game-changer for survivability and advanced combat maneuvers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Armament Haki">
    You must first possess Armament Haki. This is a strict prerequisite for unlocking Observation Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Train Your Haki Stats">
    After acquiring Armament Haki, you need to train your overall Haki stat to **200**. This can be done by actively using your Armament Haki in combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Stamina Requirement">
    Ensure your Stamina stat also reaches **200**. This high stamina pool is necessary to sustain the sensory demands of Observation Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Travel to Desert Island">
    With your stats ready, make your way to Desert Island. This arid landscape is where the Katakuri NPC resides, who will initiate your Observation Haki trial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Locate Katakuri NPC">
    Find the Katakuri NPC on Desert Island. They are usually found in a specific, notable location.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Start the Trial">
    Speak with Katakuri to begin the Observation Haki trial. During this trial, the game will temporarily grant you Observation Haki for free.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Complete the Trial">
    The trial typically involves a simple task, such as getting hit a certain number of times (e.g., 10 times) by a boss while your temporary Observation Haki is active. This demonstrates your ability to adapt to its precognitive effects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Prerequisites">
Do not attempt the Observation Haki quest without first acquiring Armament Haki and meeting the 200 Haki and 200 Stamina stat requirements. Failing to do so will prevent you from starting the trial.
</Callout>
