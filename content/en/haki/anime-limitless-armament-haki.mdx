<Section icon={Target} title="Armament Haki Acquisition Requirements and Quest Steps">
Before you can even begin the quest for **Anime Limitless Armament Haki**, you must meet specific stat requirements. These prerequisites ensure that you have a basic level of combat proficiency. Once met, the trainer will provide a series of tasks designed to test your combat skills.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Pre-requisites">
Ensure your **Strength** and **Stamina** stats are at least **75** each. Failing to meet these will prevent you from starting the quest. These stats can be increased by simply engaging in combat and using relevant abilities.
</Callout>

**Haki Quest Breakdown:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Meet Stat Requirements">
    Increase your **Strength** and **Stamina** to at least **75**. You can do this by continuously punching enemies (for Strength) and using skills (for Stamina). Actively grinding these stats will make the quest smoother.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Perform M1 Hits">
    Land **400 M1 (basic attack) hits** on any enemies. Monkeys on Monkey Island are a convenient target. This step focuses on your basic attack proficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Skills">
    Use any of your abilities or skills **50 times**. Choose skills with short cooldowns to complete this objective quickly. Switching between multiple skills can also speed up the process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Enemies">
    Defeat **75 enemies**. For efficiency, return to the Starter Island and use your strongest Area of Effect (AOE) abilities or dash between groups of weaker enemies to quickly clear them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
Upon successful completion of all quest objectives, the Busoshoku Trainer will grant you Armament Haki. You might even get a cool purple color for your Haki!
</Callout>
