<Section icon={Target} title="How to Get Armament Haki (Busoshoku)">
Acquiring Armament Haki is the first step in your Haki journey. This powerful ability allows you to harden your body, increasing your defensive capabilities and enabling you to hit opponents who would otherwise be intangible, such as Logia-type users. The quest for Armament Haki is straightforward but requires some initial stat investment.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Pre-requisites">
Do not attempt the Armament Haki quest without first meeting the 75 Strength and 75 Stamina requirements. Failing to do so will prevent you from starting the quest, wasting your travel time.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Stat Requirements">
    Before heading to the trainer, ensure your character has at least **75 Strength** and **75 Stamina**. These stats can be increased by simply punching enemies or using abilities. The "auto M1s" setting in your game options is highly recommended for passive stat grinding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Busoshoku Trainer">
    Travel to **Monkey Island**, which is the second island in the game. Once there, navigate to the back and then to the left side of the island. You will find the **Busoshoku trainer** NPC.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate the Quest">
    Speak to the Busoshoku trainer. If you meet the stat requirements, he will offer you a quest. The quest involves three main objectives:
    - Land **400 M1 hits** (basic attacks).
    - Use any skill **50 times**.
    - Defeat **75 enemies**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Quest Objectives">
    - **400 M1 Hits**: Simply attack any NPC or enemy 400 times. Monkeys on Monkey Island are good targets.
    - **50 Skill Uses**: Use your skills repeatedly. Choose a skill with a short cooldown for faster completion.
    - **Defeat 75 Enemies**: Return to the starter island or find a dense group of low-level enemies. Use your most powerful AoE abilities to quickly clear groups.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Claim Your Haki">
    After completing all objectives, return to the Busoshoku trainer. He will grant you **Armament Haki**. You will also receive a random Haki color, which can be customized later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Get Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku)">
Observation Haki is an advanced form of Haki that grants you enhanced senses, allowing you to predict and dodge incoming attacks. This Haki is a game-changer for survivability and requires you to have already unlocked and trained your Armament Haki.

<Callout type="success" title="Evasion Master">
Observation Haki is your key to becoming an evasion master. It's particularly useful against powerful bosses and in PvP, allowing you to avoid critical damage and maintain pressure on your opponents.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Train Armament Haki to 200 Stats">
    Before you can even think about Observation Haki, your **Armament Haki must be trained to 200 stats**. This means actively using Armament Haki during combat to level it up. The more you use it, the faster it will train.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Meet Additional Stat Requirements">
    In addition to the Haki stat, you will need **200 Stamina**. Continue grinding your stamina by punching and using abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Travel to Desert Island">
    Once your Armament Haki is at 200 stats and you have 200 Stamina, head to **Desert Island**. This is typically the third main island in the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Find Katakuri NPC">
    On Desert Island, locate the **Katakuri NPC**. He is the trainer for Observation Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Start and Complete the Trial">
    Speak to Katakuri and begin his trial. During the trial, you will automatically be granted Observation Haki for a limited time. Your task is to get hit **10 times by a boss** while Observation Haki is active. This trial is generally considered easy to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Unlock Observation Haki">
    Upon successfully completing Katakuri's trial, you will permanently unlock **Observation Haki**. You can then begin training it to further enhance its effects.
  </Step>
</Steps>
