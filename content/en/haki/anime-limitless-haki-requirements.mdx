<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Armament Haki">
Armament Haki is your first step into the world of advanced combat techniques in Anime Limitless. It allows you to harden your body, enhancing both your offensive and defensive capabilities. The process involves meeting specific stat requirements and completing a series of tasks.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Check">
Ensure your **Strength** and **Stamina** stats are at least **75** before attempting to start the Armament Haki quest. Grinding these stats beforehand will save you time.
</Callout>

### Armament Haki Requirements

| Requirement         | Value               | How to Obtain                                    |
| :------------------ | :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------- |
| **Stamina**         | 75                  | Punching, using skills, specific quests          |
| **Strength**        | 75                  | Punching, using melee weapons, specific quests   |
| **Location**        | Monkey Island       | Busoshoku Trainer NPC                            |
| **Quest Steps**     | 3                   | See detailed steps below                         |
| **Completion Time** | ~10-20 minutes      | Varies based on preparation and efficiency       |

### Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Armament Haki

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Monkey Island">
    Travel to **Monkey Island**, which is usually the second island you'll encounter after the starter island. Once there, navigate to the back-left side of the island to find the **Busoshoku Trainer NPC**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Meet Stat Requirements">
    Before the trainer offers you the quest, you must have at least **75 Strength** and **75 Stamina**. These stats can be increased by simply fighting enemies, using melee attacks, or performing skills. If you're short on stats, spend some time grinding on the starter island or Monkey Island's weaker enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Quest">
    The Busoshoku Trainer will give you a quest with three parts:
    1. **Land 400 M1 hits**: Equip a melee weapon or use your fists and attack any enemies. Monkeys on Monkey Island are a good target.
    2. **Use any skill 50 times**: Spam a skill with a short cooldown.
    3. **Defeat 75 enemies**: Return to the starter island or find groups of weaker enemies and use your strongest AoE abilities to quickly clear them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Your Haki">
    After completing all three objectives, return to the Busoshoku Trainer. They will grant you **Armament Haki**. You can now activate it and begin training its proficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quick Tip for M1 Hits">
Enable the "Auto M1s" setting in your game options. This allows your character to continuously attack, making the 400 M1 hits quest much faster, especially if you leave your character grinding overnight.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Unlocking Observation Haki">
Once you've mastered Armament Haki, the next step is to unlock Observation Haki. This advanced ability grants you heightened senses, allowing you to predict and dodge attacks, making you incredibly agile in combat.

<Callout type="info" title="Prerequisite">
You **must** have Armament Haki unlocked and its stat trained to at least **200** before you can even begin the quest for Observation Haki.
</Callout>

### Observation Haki Prerequisites

| Requirement         | Value               | Status                                           |
| :------------------ | :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------- |
| **Armament Haki**   | Unlocked            | Essential prerequisite                           |
| **Haki Stat**       | 200                 | Train your Armament Haki by using it in combat   |
| **Stamina**         | 200                 | Continue grinding this stat                      |
| **Location**        | Desert Island       | Katakuri NPC                                     |
| **Quest Type**      | Trial               | Focuses on dodging and survival                  |

### Steps to Acquire Observation Haki

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Train Armament Haki Stat to 200">
    Continuously use your Armament Haki in combat. Every time you activate it or engage in battle while it's active, your Haki stat will increase. Grind enemies until your Haki stat reaches **200**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach Desert Island">
    Travel to **Desert Island**, which is typically the third major island. Look for the **Katakuri NPC**. He is the one who will initiate the Observation Haki trial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Meet Stat Requirements">
    Speak to Katakuri. You will need **200 Stamina** and **200 Haki stats** (from your Armament Haki training) to start his trial. If you haven't met these, return to grinding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Observation Trial">
    Katakuri will grant you temporary Observation Haki for the trial. The task is straightforward: you need to get hit by the boss **10 times** while Observation Haki is active. This tests your ability to react and dodge, even if you intentionally take some hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock Observation Haki">
    Upon successfully completing the trial, Katakuri will permanently unlock Observation Haki for you. You can now use its dodging abilities to improve your survivability in all combat scenarios.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
For the Observation Haki trial, focus on staying alive while taking the required hits. The goal isn't to defeat the boss, but to demonstrate your understanding of evasion.
</Callout>
