<Section icon={Swords} title="Acquiring Armament Haki: The Prerequisite">
Before you can even begin your journey to obtain **Anime Limitless Observation Haki**, you must first acquire and train Armament Haki. This is a crucial step that many players overlook, leading to frustration later on.

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Skip Armament Haki!">
Attempting to get Observation Haki without first obtaining and adequately training Armament Haki will be impossible. The game's progression system explicitly requires Armament Haki as a prerequisite.
</Callout>

Here's how to get Armament Haki:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Travel to Monkey Island">
    Your first destination is Monkey Island. This is where the Busoshoku trainer resides.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Busoshoku Trainer">
    Once on Monkey Island, follow the path to find the Busoshoku trainer NPC.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Meet Stat Requirements">
    To start the Armament Haki quest, you need a minimum of **75 Stamina** and **75 Strength or Sword** stats. Ensure these are met before interacting with the trainer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Quest">
    The quest itself is relatively straightforward and shouldn't take long, typically around 10-20 minutes. Follow the trainer's instructions to successfully acquire Armament Haki.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Armament Haki allows you to harden your body and weapons, increasing your attack power and defense. It's a foundational skill for any serious fighter in Anime Limitless.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking Anime Limitless Observation Haki">
Once Armament Haki is yours and sufficiently trained, you can embark on the quest for **Anime Limitless Observation Haki**. This advanced form of Haki is invaluable for dodging attacks and gaining foresight in battle.

<Callout type="success" title="Observation Haki Benefits">
Observation Haki allows you to predict your opponent's moves, making it possible to **dodge attacks automatically**. This can be a game-changer in both PvE and PvP scenarios, significantly boosting your survivability and combat effectiveness.
</Callout>

Here’s a detailed breakdown of the steps:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Train Your Armament Haki">
    Before seeking Observation Haki, you must train your existing Armament Haki to at least **200 stats**. This is a hard requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Travel to Desert Island">
    Your next destination is Desert Island. This island hosts the NPC crucial for the Observation Haki quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Katakuri NPC">
    On Desert Island, locate the Katakuri NPC. He is your gateway to learning Observation Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Meet Observation Haki Stat Requirements">
    To begin Katakuri's trial, you need **200 Stamina** and **200 Haki** stats. These are separate from the Armament Haki requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Start the Trial">
    Talk to Katakuri and start the trial. During the trial, you will automatically be granted Observation Haki for free.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Complete the Trial Challenge">
    The trial requires you to turn on Observation Haki and get hit approximately **10 times** by a boss. This is generally considered an easy task.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
After acquiring Observation Haki, remember that it can be **trained and improved over time** simply by using it. The more you activate and rely on it in combat, the stronger it will become.
</Callout>
