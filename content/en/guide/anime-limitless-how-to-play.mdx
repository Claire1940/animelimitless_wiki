Progression in **anime limitless** is a blend of traditional leveling and focused stat grinding. Your total power level is a sum of all your stats, making targeted training crucial.

### Leveling System Explained
Each island typically has quests that allow you to grind specific stats. For instance, holding a sword and attacking will level up your sword stat. Similarly, using melee or a gun will train those respective stats. The game guides you through this process with a 'track quest' system.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Strength/Sword Early">
    Focus on leveling up your **Strength** or **Sword** stat first. These are often the easiest to grind initially and provide a solid foundation for combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Track Quests">
    Open your menu and select 'Track Quest' to see which islands offer quests for specific stats. This helps you efficiently target your grinding efforts.

  <Step num="3" title="Enable Auto-Grinding Features">

  <Step num="4" title="Level Health and Stamina">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Haki and Fighting Styles">
Effective combat in **anime limitless** relies heavily on mastering Haki and choosing a suitable fighting style. These elements provide crucial damage multipliers and defensive capabilities.

### Understanding Haki
There are two main types of Haki: Armament Haki and Observation Haki. Both are essential for advanced combat.

| Haki Type         | Effect                                       | Unlock Requirement                               | Location      |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------- | :------------ |
| **Armament Haki** | Hardens body for increased attack and defense| 75 Stamina, 75 Strength/Sword                    | Monkey Island |
| **Observation Haki**| Allows dodging attacks, increases dodge count| Armament Haki (200 stats), 200 Stamina           | Desert Island |

**Steps to Obtain Armament Haki:**
1. Travel to **Monkey Island**.
2. Locate and talk to the **Busoshoku Trainer**.
3. Ensure you have at least **75 Stamina** and **75 Strength or Sword** stats.
4. Accept and complete the quest provided by the trainer.

**Steps to Obtain Observation Haki:**
1. First, acquire **Armament Haki** and train it to **200 stats**.
2. Head to **Desert Island**.
3. Find the **Katakuri NPC** and talk to him.
4. You will need **200 Stamina** and **200 Haki** to start his trial.
5. During the trial, turn on Observation Haki (it will be given to you for free temporarily) and dodge 10 attacks.

<Callout type="tip" title="Haki Training">
Observation Haki trains as you use it to dodge attacks. The more you use it, the higher your dodge count will become, up to a maximum of nine dodges.
</Callout>

### The Abyss: True End-Game Challenge
The Abyss is the ultimate challenge in **anime limitless**, reserved for only the strongest players. It offers the best armor and materials for crafting the most powerful weapon in the game.

**Steps to Access the Abyss:**
1. Reach **Truffle Island**.
2. Climb to the designated area where the **Abyss Gatekeeper** NPC is located.
3. You must have a **Total Power of 3,000** (sum of all your stats).
4. If your power is insufficient, the gatekeeper will reject you, stating, "The abyss rejects the weak. Beyond this gate stands the one who took it all. Return when your power total reaches 3,000."

Behind the Abyss gate lies a boss that drops the best armor and a crafter for the best weapon. Acquiring this gear signifies true mastery of the game.
