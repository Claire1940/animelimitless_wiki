<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Acquiring and Utilizing Souls">
Souls are powerful special abilities that significantly impact your build in Anime Limitless. Obtaining strong Souls is a major part of progression, as they can drastically change your combat performance and strategic options. Souls have various rarities, from Common to Legendary, with higher rarities offering more potent effects.

There are two primary methods for acquiring Souls, each with its own challenges and rewards. Understanding these methods is crucial for optimizing your character's power.

| Source | Method | Cost/Requirement | Rarity Range | Notes |
| :----- | :----- | :--------------- | :----------- | :---- |
| **Boss Drops** | Defeat bosses, chance to drop chests | Boss difficulty | Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary | Unpredictable, but free. Legendary Souls are rare. |
| **Soul Dealer** | Purchase from NPC in West City | 25,000 Gold per roll | Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary | Consistent access, but requires significant gold investment. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Soul Spawns & Despawns">
Souls naturally **spawn around the world every 30 minutes** and will **despawn after 20 minutes** if not collected. Be vigilant and explore frequently to secure rare powers before they disappear or other players find them.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Boss Farming for Chests">
    Locate and defeat various bosses across different islands. These bosses have a chance to drop chests upon defeat. Open these chests for a chance to receive a Soul. This method is free but relies on luck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visiting the Soul Dealer">
    Travel to **West City** and find the Soul Dealer NPC. You can purchase Souls from this dealer for **25,000 Gold each**. This provides a more consistent, albeit costly, way to obtain new Souls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Soul Resetting">
    If you're not satisfied with your current Soul, you can reset it at West City for **150 Gems**. Gems are typically acquired from quests or fighting bosses. This allows for build optimization without starting from scratch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering Haki Abilities">
Haki abilities are essential for both offense and defense in Anime Limitless, providing significant advantages in combat. There are two main types of Haki: Armament Haki and Observation Haki. Each offers unique benefits and requires specific training to master.

| Haki Type | Location to Learn | Requirements | Primary Benefit | Training Method |
| :-------- | :---------------- | :----------- | :-------------- | :-------------- |
| **Armament** | Monkey Island (Busoshoku Trainer) | 75 Stamina, 75 Strength/Sword | Increased defense, attack power | Use it in combat; quest from trainer |
| **Observation** | Desert Island (Katakuri NPC) | Armament Haki, 200 Haki stats, 200 Stamina | Dodge attacks, enhanced awareness | Get hit 10 times by boss with it active |

<Callout type="info" title="Haki Training Tip">
Observation Haki allows you to dodge incoming attacks, a critical skill for surviving tougher encounters. Once you acquire it, actively use it in combat to level it up, making your character much more agile.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Exploring Rifts and the Abyss">
Rifts and the Abyss represent some of the most challenging and rewarding content in Anime Limitless. These areas are designed for players seeking powerful gear, evolution materials, and ultimate challenges. Understanding their purpose and requirements is key to progressing into the endgame.

| Feature | Location | Purpose | Key Drops | Requirements | Notes |
| :------ | :------- | :------ | :-------- | :----------- | :---- |
| **Kid Buu Rift** | West City (corner) | Obtain items for Soul evolution, accessories, cores | Soul evolution materials, accessories | Varies by boss | One of two legendary rifts |
| **Goku SSJ4 Rift** | Truffle Island | Obtain items for Soul evolution, accessories, cores | Soul evolution materials, accessories | Varies by boss | One of two legendary rifts |
| **The Abyss** | Truffle Island (Abyss Gatekeeper) | Best armor, crafter for best weapon | Best armor, weapon crafting materials | 3,000 Total Power | Endgame content, extremely difficult |

<Callout type="success" title="Endgame Gear Strategy">
The Abyss offers the best armor and materials for crafting the best weapon in the game. While reaching 3,000 Total Power is a significant grind, the rewards are unparalleled and essential for maximizing your character's potential.
</Callout>

The **Abyss** is explicitly designed for endgame players. To even enter, you need a combined **3,000 Total Power** across all your stats. This is a formidable goal that will require extensive grinding and optimization of your build. Upon entering, you'll face a boss that drops the game's best armor and encounter a crafter for what is believed to be the best weapon, requiring rare cores from across the game.
