Anime Limitless deviates from standard clicker-simulators by requiring active stat mastery. Your total power is a combination of Strength, Sword, Soul, Gun, Defense, and Haki. To reach the end-game content, you must strategically balance these stats.

The game provides a "Track Quest" feature in the UI, which is essential for navigation. This tool highlights exactly which island you should visit to level up specific stats. <Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Styles and Equipment Progression">

In Anime Limitless, your power comes from four main sources: Melee, Weapons, Souls, and Guns. While weapons are the most common early-game choice, **Souls** (the game's version of Devil Fruits or Stand powers) are the true end-game goal.

### Sword Progression Path
Swords are reliable and offer consistent DPS. You should follow this upgrade path to ensure you are never underpowered for your current island.

| Weapon | Location | Requirement/Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Katana** | Starter Island | Purchase from Merchant |
| **Axehand** | Monkey Island | Drop from Axehand Boss |
| **Two-Sword Style** | West City | Boss Summoner (Gems/Essence) |
| **Dark Blade** | West City | 10% Drop from Mihawk Boss |
| **Abyss Sword** | Truffle Island | **End-game Rift** reward |

### Obtaining and Awakening Souls
Souls are the most powerful assets in your arsenal. There are three ways to obtain them:
1. **Soul Dealer**: Roll for a random soul in West City for 25,000 Cash.
2. **Boss Chests**: Every boss has a chance to drop a chest containing a soul.
3. **Map Spawns**: Souls occasionally spawn inside chests hidden around the world.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Souls consume significant stamina. Before switching to a Soul-heavy build, ensure your **Stamina stat** is at least level 150 to avoid being left defenseless mid-combat.
</Callout>

The current meta highlights **Kid Buu** and **SSJ4 Goku** as the strongest souls due to their full transformation modes. These transformations provide massive stat boosts and unique skill sets that are essential for clearing the Truffle Island Rift.
