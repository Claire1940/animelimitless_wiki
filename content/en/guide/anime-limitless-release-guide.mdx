### Acquiring and Resetting Souls

Souls are powerful entities that grant unique abilities and transformations. Obtaining the right soul can drastically change your gameplay experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Boss Chest Drops">
    Defeat island bosses and super bosses. They have a chance to drop chests, which can contain souls of various rarities (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary). This is a primary method for obtaining powerful souls like Super Saiyan 4 Goku.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Soul Dealer">
    Visit the **Soul Dealer** in West City. For 25,000 cash per roll, you can attempt to acquire a soul. This method offers a direct way to get souls if you have ample cash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Map Spawns">
    Keep an eye out for **Soul Chests** that can randomly spawn across the map. These chests also have a chance to grant you a soul.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Soul Resetting:**
If you wish to reset your soul, head to West City. On the left side of the main building, you'll find an NPC for soul reset. It costs **150 Gems** to reset your soul, allowing you to re-roll for a different one or adjust your build. Gems are typically acquired through quests and defeating bosses.
</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Haki and Advanced Combat Techniques">
Haki is a vital combat mechanic that significantly enhances your offensive and defensive capabilities. There are two main types: Armament Haki and Observation Haki.

### Obtaining Armament Haki

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Travel to Monkey Island">
    Locate the **Busoshoku Trainer** on Monkey Island.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Meet Requirements">
    You'll need **75 Stamina** and either **75 Strength** or **75 Sword** stat points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Quest">
    Talk to the trainer to start and complete the quest. This quest is relatively quick, usually taking 10-20 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Obtaining Observation Haki

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prerequisite: Armament Haki">
    You must first possess Armament Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Train Haki Stat">
    Increase your Haki stat to at least **200 points**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Travel to Desert Island">
    Go to Desert Island (located behind Starter Island).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Find Katakuri NPC">
    Locate the **Katakuri NPC** and talk to him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Meet Requirements & Start Trial">
    You'll need **200 Stamina** and **200 Haki**. Begin the trial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Complete Trial">
    During the trial, turn on Observation Haki (it's given for free during the trial) and get hit by the boss approximately 10 times. This is a very easy task to complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Haki Benefits">
- **Armament Haki:** Enhances your attacks, allowing you to hit elemental users and deal more damage.
- **Observation Haki:** Grants the ability to dodge attacks more effectively. Train it by consistently using it in combat to increase your dodge count (max 9 dodges, starting with 2).
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Endgame Content: Rifts and The Abyss">
For seasoned players seeking the ultimate challenge and rewards, Anime Limitless offers challenging endgame content in the form of Rifts and the formidable Abyss.

### Rifts

Rifts are special zones containing powerful bosses and unique drops, essential for evolving souls, obtaining accessories, and gathering cores. Currently, there are two legendary rifts:

| Rift Type | Location | Awakener Location | Primary Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Goku SSJ4 Rift** | Truffle Island | In front of quest giver on Truffle Island | Obtain items for SSJ4 Soul evolution, accessories. |
| **Kid Buu Rift** | West City (corner) | Opposite side of West City | Obtain items for Kid Buu Soul evolution, accessories. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Rift Rewards">
Rifts are your go-to for powerful items. They drop essentials for **Soul Evolution**, rare **Accessories**, and crucial **Cores** needed for crafting the best gear. Make sure to tackle them with a strong team.
</Callout>

### The Abyss

The Abyss represents the ultimate endgame challenge, designed for the most powerful players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Truffle Island">
    Travel to Truffle Island, the location of the Abyss Gatekeeper.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Abyss Gatekeeper">
    Climb to the designated area on Truffle Island to find the Abyss Gatekeeper NPC.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Achieve 3,000 Total Power">
    To enter, your **total power level** (sum of all stats) must reach 3,000. The Gatekeeper will reject the weak, stating, "The abyss rejects the weak. Beyond this gate stands the one who took it all. Return when your power total reaches 3,000."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Conquer the Abyss">
    Inside, you'll face a powerful boss that drops the **best armor in the game**. There's also a crafter for what is believed to be the **best weapon**, requiring all cores in the game to craft.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="The Abyss: Not for the Faint of Heart">
The Abyss is exclusively for high-level players. Ensure your combined stats yield a **Total Power of 3,000** before attempting entry, as it houses the toughest challenges and most coveted gear in **Anime Limitless**.
</Callout>
