<Section icon={Swords} title="Power Progression: Stats, Souls, and Weapons">
In Anime Limitless, your power comes from a combination of farmed stats, powerful souls, and well-chosen weapons. Understanding how to acquire and enhance each of these elements is crucial for becoming a formidable player.

### Stat Progression Guide

Stats are a core aspect of your character's power. You'll need to actively farm them, and the game provides tools to help.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Strength & Sword">
    Begin by leveling Strength and Sword. The "Track Quest" feature will guide you to islands suitable for farming these stats. Auto M1 and Auto Skill are your best friends here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire a Soul">
    Once you have a soul, integrate it into your farming routine. Souls provide unique abilities and often have their own progression paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level Health & Stamina">
    Health increases by taking damage, or by standing in water if you're a soul user. Stamina and Haki build up through using skills and weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Haki & Observation Haki">
    **Armament Haki:** Head to Monkey Island. You'll need 75 Stamina and 75 Strength/Sword to start the quest.
    **Observation Haki:** Once your Haki reaches level 200, go to Sand Island and talk to Kosakuri. Complete his trial (10 dodges) to unlock it. This significantly increases your dodge count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Mastery vs. Skill Unlock">
Mastery primarily tracks your progress and increases damage every 50 levels. Skills are unlocked by reaching specific stat requirements, not mastery. Future updates will introduce skill trees where mastery will play a more significant role.
</Callout>
